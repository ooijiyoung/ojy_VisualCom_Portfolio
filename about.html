Executive Summary
Altius Alpha is a Malaysian-based international interior design studio that provides the finest and most luxurious services for both residential and commercial projects. Altius Alpha consists of Ooi Ji Young, Tan Jin Soon, Alwyn Yeo Khai Yuan & Lim Yen Er that are very passionate about interior design and they aim to be the most innovative interior design studios.

Altius Alpha’s interior design will satisfy and captivate the customer’s taste of design. They will prepare a survey for the customer and it will allow the designers to make a unique design to satisfy their customers. Altius Alpha uses very high quality products to design the most sophisticated design.

Altius Alpha are based on furniture manufacturer, product and interior fitting out specialist. The company’s core competitive advantage lies in its ability to utilize a myriad of latest technologies, innovative and ideas to produce premium panelling products of unrivalled quality and workmanship. Exclusive distrinutorship of renowned imported brands and the export of two widely received in-house brands have been customised to reach a comprehensive spectrum of residential and commercial development across major countries around the worlds.
